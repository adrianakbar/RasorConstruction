Company Profile

PT Rasor Construction was established in 1990, aiming to provide top-quality construction equipment and services. Over the years, we have grown to become a leading name in the industry, known for our commitment to excellence and innovation.

"Building the Future, Restoring the Past"

Our vision is to be the foremost construction company in the region, setting benchmarks in quality, safety, and sustainability. We aim to achieve this through continuous improvement, innovative practices, and a dedicated workforce.

Looking ahead, PT Rasor Construction plans to expand its operations globally, adopting advanced technologies and sustainable practices to meet the evolving demands of the construction industry. Our future endeavors include diversifying our services and entering new markets.
